💡 Debut prep tips

  • Commission your model + rigging first. They have the longest queues — good riggers book out 2–6 months. Everything else (panels, emotes, overlays) can be made while you wait.
  • Always check the artist's Terms of Service before paying — turnaround time, revision count, commercial use rights, and whether streaming the WIP is okay.
  • Budget tip: you don't need everything at debut. Model, one overlay set, panels, a PFP, and a schedule graphic are plenty. Emotes and sub badges unlock after affiliate anyway.
  • Pay with tracked methods (PayPal invoice, not friends & family) so you're protected if a commission falls through.
  • Do a full tech rehearsal a few days early: model tracking, alerts, transitions, audio — the whole stream, recorded, watched back. Debut-day surprises are the worst surprises.
  • Announce 2–3 weeks out with a teaser, then countdown posts. Your debut works best as a small event people can plan to attend.

🎀 The checklist
