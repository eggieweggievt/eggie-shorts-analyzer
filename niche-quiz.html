Niche Finder 🔍

Your niche isn't just the game you play — it's what you do × the energy you bring. Two streamers can play the same game and be completely different channels. This quiz finds both halves of yours.

🌷 The two halves of a niche

Part 1 — your content: the thing you actually do on stream. Not just "gaming" — horror nights, retro deep-cuts, cozy farming sims, karaoke, cooking, study-with-me… the specific corner where people find you.

Part 2 — your vibe: the energy you give the room. Chaotic gremlin, cozy campfire, sparkly-wholesome, deadpan and dry, theatrical lore-keeper… the reason people stay after the game ends. The vibe is the part nobody can copy.

Fifteen quick questions, answered honestly (not aspirationally). You'll get your stream recipe — content × vibe — with ideas to test-drive it this week. No sign-in needed.
