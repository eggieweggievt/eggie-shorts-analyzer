Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about how the Creator Hub works, how your data is handled, what it costs, and what the Optimizer knows. 🐙

✨ The short version

  • The hub is completely free. There's no paywall, trial timer, or locked features.
  • It is not generative AI and is not connected to any LLM such as ChatGPT.
  • Your saved data is encrypted and stored in a private database. It is never sold or shared without your permission.
  • The Optimizer processes your video locally in your browser — the video itself is never uploaded.
  • Under the hood it's a large database plus pattern recognition, with speech-to-text for transcription — essentially pre-AI technology.

🌸 Getting started

What is Eggie's Creator Hub?

A free suite of tools to help VTubers and content creators plan, write, and grow their content — built by one creator trying to make something that genuinely helps others.

What does the Optimizer give me?

Upload a short, tell it what the short is about (or let it read the visuals and audio), and you get:

🎯 A score out of 10

Broken down by hook, duration, title, hashtags, caption, engagement, SEO, niche fit, and retention — sorted by what moves the score most.

🏷️ Five smart hashtags

A 1 small / 2 medium / 2 large mix drawn from a pool matched to your specific content — not generic "#shorts" filler.

📋 A ready-to-copy post pack

Title + description for YouTube, a caption for TikTok and Instagram, and a 280-character tweet for X. Hashtags editable before you copy.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. Everything runs right in your browser — just open a tool and start using it.

Can I be a beta tester?

Yes, and that's hugely appreciated. The easiest way is to reach out on my socials and let me know, ideally with your channel links so I can find you. The more creators trying the tools, the better they become.

🔒 Your data & privacy

Is the information I enter being stored somewhere?

The parts you save to your account — projects, plans, settings — are stored so they're still there when you return. That data is kept encrypted in a private database (hosted on Supabase) with as much security applied to it as reasonably possible. It is not stored in the open or left publicly accessible.

Separately, the Optimizer processes your video entirely in your browser — see "Does my video get uploaded?" below.

Does my video get uploaded anywhere?local

No. The Optimizer runs entirely in your browser. The detection models load locally and process the video frames on your own computer — the video file is never uploaded to a server. The first analysis downloads the models (around 380MB total), after which they're cached in your browser so later runs are faster and fully offline.

Do you sell my data?never

No. Your data is never sold, rented, or traded — not to advertisers, not to anyone. This is a project built by one creator to help other creators, not a data business. If that ever changed, it would be communicated clearly and in advance, but it is not something this project does.

Who is able to see my data?

By default, only you. Your content is tied to your own account through database access rules. The only time anything is shared is when you deliberately choose to share it — for example, inviting an editor or manager to a project, or publishing a media kit to a public link. Nothing is shared unless you opt in.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete individual projects and content from within the hub at any time. If you would like your entire account and all associated data removed, contact me through my socials and it will be taken care of. Your data belongs to you.

Is there a Terms of Service / Privacy Policy?

A formal Terms of Service is being written and will be linked here once it's ready. In the meantime, the plain-language summary near the bottom of this page ("How your data is handled") describes exactly what is stored, how it is protected, and what will never be done with it.

⚙️ How it actually works

Is this generative AI? Does it use ChatGPT or an LLM?

No. The hub is not connected to any LLM. It is not ChatGPT, it is not a chatbot, and it does not generate content from a language model.

While it would be technically possible to connect it to such a service, that is deliberately not how it is built. The goal is the opposite: results that are predictable, transparent, and grounded in real creator data rather than produced by a model.

So what is it, then?

Think of it as pre-AI technology — the kind of system that existed before the recent wave of generative AI: a large database combined with pattern recognition.

In practical terms, it is a very large database connected to Supabase, drawing on data from VidIQ and a range of other creator resources. The tools then identify patterns in that data — which kinds of titles, topics, and formats tend to perform well — and surface those patterns to you.

So when the Title Generator offers suggestions, it is drawing on proven patterns in real performance data, not inventing sentences from a language model.

You mentioned transcription — isn't that AI?

For converting audio into text, the hub uses a speech-to-text model (in this case Whisper). This is a transcription tool — comparable to high-quality automatic captions. It listens and writes down what was said.

That is a different category of technology from a generative chatbot. It does not make decisions, form opinions, or write content for you; it simply turns speech into text so the other tools have something to read.

How does the Optimizer actually detect what's in my video?

It combines several local models, then weighs what you tell it above what the video shows:

👁️ Florence-2 vision (×3 frames)

Samples 3 frames — opening (25%), middle (50%), closing (75%) — and runs both a scene caption and an OCR pass on each, so it catches both the visuals and any on-screen text (captions, "POV:" overlays, chat boxes, alerts). Duplicate overlays are deduped.

🎙️ Whisper transcription (medium.en)

Transcribes whatever's being said. The medium.en model is much better than tiny models at slang, character names, fast speech, and storytime phrasing.

🎞️ Frame + audio analysis

Motion/cut detection, audio loudness, BlazeFace face detection on the middle frame, colour vibe, and aspect ratio — used to score your hook strength.

📝 Your typed text + pills (tier-1)

What you type and the short-type pills you pick always win over what the video shows. Emojis become keywords (💀 → funny, 🥺 → wholesome, 🍵 → drama) and any #hashtags you add are read as explicit signals.

What's the 1S / 2M / 2L hashtag formula?

Every short gets 5 hashtags, mixed by reach size, so your video has a fighting chance at every stage of the algorithm:

S 1 small (under 500k posts) — low competition, easier to rank M 2 medium (500k–1M) — balanced reach L 2 large (1M+) — high exposure if you break out

If a topic doesn't have tags in every size, the picker fills with the best mix it can and tells you when it didn't hit the formula exactly. The 5 picks also come from different slots (primary topic → VTuber identity → content type → sub-topic) so the mix stays varied rather than clustering on one theme.

Is this affiliated with YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or VidIQ?

No. The growth rubric is based on publicly known short-form best practices plus outlier research into hashtags that actually perform. The tag pools are refreshed regularly using VidIQ data, but the hub is an independent project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any of those platforms.

📜 How your data is handled

A full Terms of Service is on the way. Until then, here is a clear, plain-language summary of how your data is treated.

📦 What is stored

Only what is needed to run the tools for you:

  • Your account information, so you can sign back in
  • The content you create here — projects, plans, titles, notes, and settings
  • Basic technical data needed to keep the tools functioning

Your video files are not part of this — the Optimizer processes them locally in your browser. Public creator-trend data (from VidIQ and similar sources) is kept separate from your personal account data.

🔐 How it is protected

Your data is encrypted and stored in a private database with access controls, so only your account can reach your content. As much security is applied as is reasonably possible for an independently run project.

🚫 What will never be done

  • Your data will not be sold — not to advertisers, not to anyone.
  • It will not be rented or traded.
  • It will not be shared without your permission. Sharing only happens when you choose it (for example, inviting an editor or publishing a media kit).
  • It will not be used to train a generative AI or LLM. The tools are not built on an LLM to begin with.

✅ Your rights

You can view, edit, and delete your content at any time, and you can request full account deletion by contacting me. Your data belongs to you.
