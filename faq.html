Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about how the Creator Hub works, how your data is handled, and what it costs. 🐙

✨ The short version

  • The hub is completely free. There's no paywall, trial timer, or locked features.
  • It is not generative AI and is not connected to any LLM such as ChatGPT.
  • Your data is encrypted and stored in a private database. It is never sold or shared without your permission.
  • Under the hood it's a large database plus pattern recognition, with speech-to-text for transcription — essentially pre-AI technology.

🔒 Your data & privacy

Is the information I enter being stored somewhere?

Yes. In order for your projects, plans, and settings to still be there when you return, they have to be saved. Your data is stored encrypted in a private database (hosted on Supabase) with as much security applied to it as reasonably possible. It is not stored in the open or left publicly accessible.

Do you sell my data?never

No. Your data is never sold, rented, or traded — not to advertisers, not to anyone. This is a project built by one creator to help other creators, not a data business. If that ever changed, it would be communicated clearly and in advance, but it is not something this project does.

Who is able to see my data?

By default, only you. Your content is tied to your own account through database access rules. The only time anything is shared is when you deliberately choose to share it — for example, inviting an editor or manager to a project, or publishing a media kit to a public link. Nothing is shared unless you opt in.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete individual projects and content from within the hub at any time. If you would like your entire account and all associated data removed, contact me through my socials and it will be taken care of. Your data belongs to you.

Is there a Terms of Service / Privacy Policy?

A formal Terms of Service is being written and will be linked here once it's ready. In the meantime, the plain-language summary further down this page ("How your data is handled") describes exactly what is stored, how it is protected, and what will never be done with it.

⚙️ How it actually works

Is this generative AI? Does it use ChatGPT or an LLM?/gen

No. The hub is not connected to any LLM. It is not ChatGPT, it is not a chatbot, and it does not generate content from a language model.

While it would be technically possible to connect it to such a service, that is deliberately not how it is built. The goal is the opposite: results that are predictable, transparent, and grounded in real creator data rather than produced by a model.

So what is it, then?

Think of it as pre-AI technology — the kind of system that existed before the recent wave of generative AI: a large database combined with pattern recognition.

In practical terms, it is a very large database connected to Supabase, drawing on data from VidIQ and a range of other creator resources. The tools then identify patterns in that data — which kinds of titles, topics, and formats tend to perform well — and surface those patterns to you.

So when the Title Generator offers suggestions, it is drawing on proven patterns in real performance data, not inventing sentences from a language model.

You mentioned transcription — isn't that AI?

For converting audio into text, the hub uses a speech-to-text model (in this case Whisper). This is a transcription tool — comparable to high-quality automatic captions. It listens and writes down what was said.

That is a different category of technology from a generative chatbot. It does not make decisions, form opinions, or write content for you; it simply turns speech into text so the other tools have something to read.

Where does the data it learns from come from?

From creator-analytics sources such as VidIQ and other resources that measure how content performs. That is the data the pattern recognition runs on. It is not built from your private information — the public trend data and your personal account data are kept separate.

💰 Cost & support

Is it paid? Is there a catch?free

It is entirely free. Every tool and resource on the hub runs on a "support it if you'd like to" basis — there is no paywall, no locked features, and no trial timer. If something here helped you and you wish to support the project, that is genuinely appreciated, but it is never required.

If it's free, how is it funded?

Primarily out of pocket, because it's a project worth building. Optional tips help cover running costs such as the database and data sources, but the hub does not depend on them and will remain free regardless.

📜 How your data is handled

A full Terms of Service is on the way. Until then, here is a clear, plain-language summary of how your data is treated.

📦 What is stored

Only what is needed to run the tools for you:

  • Your account information, so you can sign back in
  • The content you create here — projects, plans, titles, notes, and settings
  • Basic technical data needed to keep the tools functioning

Public creator-trend data (from VidIQ and similar sources) is kept separate from your personal account data.

🔐 How it is protected

Your data is encrypted and stored in a private database with access controls, so only your account can reach your content. As much security is applied as is reasonably possible for an independently run project.

🚫 What will never be done

  • Your data will not be sold — not to advertisers, not to anyone.
  • It will not be rented or traded.
  • It will not be shared without your permission. Sharing only happens when you choose it (for example, inviting an editor or publishing a media kit).
  • It will not be used to train a generative AI or LLM. The tools are not built on an LLM to begin with.

✅ Your rights

You can view, edit, and delete your content at any time, and you can request full account deletion by contacting me. Your data belongs to you.
