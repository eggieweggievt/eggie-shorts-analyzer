Creator Check-In 🌿

Short answers are totally fine! Just checking in on how things are going 💗 Same nine questions every week, nothing is graded, nothing is shared.

• Did you hit the goal you set last week?
• What got in the way, if anything?
🎯 Last check-in's goal:
• What felt good this week?
• Even something small counts!
• What did you post this week?
• What performed better or worse than you expected?
• Anything you'd do differently?
• How has chat/community been?
• Any moments that stood out — good or bad?
• How are you feeling compared to last week?
• Better, worse, about the same?
• What's still feeling hard?
• Anything new come up this week?
• What's the plan?
• Anything you're nervous or excited about?
• What would actually help you right now?
• What's the ONE thing you want to have done by next check-in?
Sealing marks the week done and keeps your streak going. You can still edit after.
