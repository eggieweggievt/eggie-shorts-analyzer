Creator Memory 🧠

One place that remembers who you are — your voice, your audience, your series, the words you live by and the ones you ban. Every tool reads from it to stay on-brand and sound like you instead of guessing. Borrowed from Odysseus' memory idea, rebuilt for your hub.

You + your brand

The basics any assistant (or collaborator) should know before writing a single word as you.

Channel basics

Your lane, format and platforms. The Optimizer reads these to score titles + suggest hashtags that fit your channel — so set them once here.

Your voice

Tone, catchphrases, and how you use emoji. This is what makes generated copy sound like you — and the tone chips also feed the Optimizer's title scoring.

Word bank

Words to lean into, and words to never use. Your analyzer and planner can check copy against these.

Recurring series + formats

The shows and formats you make on repeat — so naming, framing and titles stay consistent.

Links worth remembering

Socials, Discord, schedule, media kit — anything you'd want an assistant to drop in or reference.

Stream day — schedule + sponsors

Used to auto-fill paste-ready stream descriptions in the Optimizer (and pitches). Set it once here.

Anything else worth remembering

Freeform facts — boundaries, lore, schedule, sponsor no-gos, inside jokes. The catch-all memory.
